FINCH Eye

The FINCH Eye is a hyperspectral imaging payload onboard a 3U nanosatellite for greenhouse gas emissions research in the Greater Toronto Area. The mission is currently in the high level design phase, where components are being sourced and first pass calculations are being performed.

Optics

Leading the design of a dispersive spectrometer pushbroom system to image atmospheric greenhouse gases. The design overview is a document I wrote that shows how the payload works, as well as details about its performance. I've also managed to secure a large sponsorship with Synopsys CODE V to use their software for payload design.
